Our Story

Nexus was born out of frustration. In 2021, our founders were working at a fast-growing startup where teams were drowning in tools. Slack for chat, Asana for tasks, Google Docs for documents, Figma for designs ‚Äì the context-switching was killing productivity.

We asked ourselves: what if there was one place where all your work could live together? Where AI could handle the busywork, and humans could focus on the creative, meaningful stuff?

That's Nexus. A unified workspace that brings your team together with AI-powered workflows, real-time collaboration, and beautiful interfaces that just work.
