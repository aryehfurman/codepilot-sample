Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan whenever you need to. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for annual Enterprise plans.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start - you can explore all features risk-free.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll be prompted to choose a paid plan. If you don't subscribe, your account becomes read-only, but your data is safely stored for 30 days.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes! We offer 50% off our Professional plan for qualified nonprofit organizations. Contact our sales team for verification and setup.

Is there a free plan available?

While we don't have a permanent free plan, our 14-day trial gives you full access to test all features before committing to a subscription.

How does billing work for team members?

You're billed per active team member each month. If you add or remove team members mid-month, we'll adjust your next invoice accordingly.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

What's included in customer support?

Starter plans include email support during business hours. Professional gets priority support, and Enterprise includes 24/7 dedicated support with a customer success manager.

Do you offer custom Enterprise pricing?

Yes! For large teams (500+ users) or specific requirements, we offer custom pricing and contracts. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs.

Is my data secure and backed up?

All plans include enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage and daily backups. Enterprise plans add advanced security features and compliance certifications.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching limits. You can upgrade anytime, or we'll help you find the right plan for your growing team's needs.
